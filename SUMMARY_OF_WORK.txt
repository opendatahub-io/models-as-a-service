📋 DEPLOYMENT STRATEGY (CRD-First Approach)
═══════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════════

Why CRD-First?
  ✓ Ensures correct CRD is in etcd before operators start
  ✓ Avoids REST mapper cache issues
  ✓ Prevents CRD field pruning
  ✓ Works regardless of embedded manifests in Docker images

Deployment Order:
  1. Apply opendatahub-operator CRDs (includes AIGateway CRD with fixes)
  2. Wait 10 seconds
  3. Deploy opendatahub-operator
  4. Wait 15 seconds
  5. Apply ai-gateway-operator CRDs
  6. Deploy ai-gateway-operator
  7. Create DSCInitialization and DataScienceCluster CRs
  8. Wait for all pods to be Running
